The Hub

A calm, anonymous student forum inside PlanIt where students can ask for help, share school or personal worries, and support others in their grade.

Students post under random names. Teachers can monitor for safety and report conduct issues, but they do not join the discussions.

Posting Guidelines

  • Be kind and respectful.
  • No swearing, insults, threats, or bullying.
  • Do not reveal private personal information.
  • Ask for help clearly so others can respond well.

Important Note

  • This demo saves posts and comments in this browser only.
  • For many students at once, you would need a real backend and database.
  • Private groups are shown as a front-end demo section here.
  • Image uploads in this version stay in the current browser storage.

How this works

  • Students can post anonymously under random names.
  • Posts can be filtered with the search bar.
  • Comments are saved and stay after refresh on this device.
  • Teachers can monitor and report posts, but cannot reply here.

Teacher Moderation

Teachers do not speak in The Hub. They only monitor and report content that breaks conduct.

Auto-flag examples Swearing, insults, targeted harassment, threats, or unsafe personal details.
Manual reports Teachers can press a report button on any post if it breaks school rules.
